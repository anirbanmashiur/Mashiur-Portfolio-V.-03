Case Study

Pedal Patch

A streamlined mobile experience for cyclists who want fast planning and clear ride insights.

Team

Academic project

Duration

8 weeks

Platform

Mobile app

Project Overview

All the essentials, one ride-ready workflow.

Pedal Patch brings route planning, ride history, and gear tracking into one clean mobile workflow. The concept focuses on fast access to routes, clear stats, and easy maintenance reminders.

The challenge

Cyclists often use multiple apps for routes, fitness, and equipment, which makes it hard to stay organized and consistent.

  • Too many tools for a single ride
  • Stats and gear notes get lost
  • Too much friction to plan quickly

Research insights

Users want a simple, dependable flow for planning rides, tracking progress, and remembering gear maintenance without deep configuration.

  • Plan a ride in under a minute
  • See key stats at a glance
  • Get lightweight maintenance reminders

The solution

A focused, card-based UI that keeps planning and tracking simple. The system favors quick actions, high contrast, and minimal steps between intent and action.

Key Features

Designed for fast decisions on the go

Quick Route Planning

Start a ride in seconds with a lightweight route picker and clear ETA/terrain details.

Ride Insights

Stats are summarized in scannable cards to highlight distance, time, and progress.

Gear & Maintenance

Keep equipment notes and service reminders tied to your ride history.

Visual direction

High-contrast cards and warm accents reinforce energy and motion without overwhelming the UI. The layout stays compact to keep attention on the next action.

Results & Validation

What improved

Faster planning

Reduced steps to plan and start a ride.

Clearer stats

Ride details surfaced in a single view.

Less clutter

Focused navigation with fewer actions per screen.

My role

UI/UX Designer

  • User flows and wireframes
  • UI system and visual design
  • Interactive prototype

Duration: 8 weeks

Takeaways

Reducing steps and emphasizing visual hierarchy created a more confident flow for riders who want quick, reliable planning.
