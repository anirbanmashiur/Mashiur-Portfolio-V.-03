Project Overview

Personalized recovery, guided day by day.

Kyntra is a mobile app focused on non-emergency injury recovery. The experience supports personalized exercise plans, progress tracking, and recovery guidance.

Due to confidentiality, only a high-level summary is shown here.

The challenge

Users needed a gentle, supportive recovery experience that reduces confusion, keeps motivation high, and adapts to changing pain levels.

  • Simple daily guidance
  • Clear progress indicators
  • Accessible, reassuring UI

Research insights

People want recovery plans that are easy to follow without medical jargon, with clear progress and low effort to start a session.

  • Short, guided routines
  • Daily encouragement
  • Visual progress cues

The solution

A calm, mobile-first interface that guides users through recovery plans with clear steps, motivational cues, and adaptive routines.

Key Features

Supportive recovery without overwhelm

Personalized Recovery Plan

Adaptive routines that adjust based on pain level and recovery stage.

Guided Exercise

Step-by-step sessions with clear visual guidance and timing.

Progress Tracking

Simple progress cues and streaks to keep users motivated.

Visual direction

Warm tones and soft layouts create reassurance, while clear hierarchy keeps the experience easy to follow.
