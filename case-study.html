Case Study

StreetSight

AI-powered billboard CRM for small agencies.

Awarded Design Excellence - Project II, Langara College

Team

4 designers, 4 developers

Duration

13 weeks

Platform

B2B SaaS - Web App

Project Overview

Clarity for agencies, visibility for campaigns.

StreetSight is a B2B SaaS web application designed to simplify billboard campaign management for small advertising agencies. The platform centralizes inventory, automates proposals, and introduces real-time analytics to an industry still dependent on manual workflows.

The challenge

Billboard advertising remains one of the most effective marketing channels, yet managing campaigns is inefficient - especially for smaller agencies.

  • Manual proposal creation and invoicing
  • Unclear pricing and lack of transparency
  • No real-time performance or ROI tracking
  • Limited access due to dominance of large distributors like Pattison Outdoor

Research insights

Research was conducted through surveys, interviews, and market analysis.

  • 70% of small agencies find billboard advertising difficult to manage
  • 60% lose time through phone calls and spreadsheets
  • #1 frustration: No visibility into campaign performance

These insights revealed a clear opportunity for a centralized, data-driven solution.

The solution

StreetSight is an AI-powered CRM platform that streamlines billboard operations by automating key workflows and providing actionable insights. The goal was to make billboard advertising simple, transparent, and measurable for smaller agencies.

Key Features

What makes StreetSight powerful

Real-Time Analytics & Mapping

Live performance data, interactive maps, and visual dashboards help agencies track impressions and identify high-performing billboard locations.

AI-Powered Proposals & Quotation

Automated proposal and quotation generation based on demographics and performance data, reducing errors and saving time.

Client & Agreement Management

Centralized client records, lease agreements, proposal tracking, and follow-ups keep campaigns organized and efficient.

Visual direction

StreetSight challenges the traditionally dull CRM aesthetic with a bright, confident color palette, clear data visualization, and Billi, a friendly mascot to humanize a B2B product. The design balances usability with personality to make complex data approachable.

Results & Validation

Early feedback and outcomes

Industry fit

Panel feedback validated strong industry fit.

Most impactful

Real-time analytics identified as the most impactful feature.

Faster proposals

Proposal workflows were significantly faster than manual methods.

Bold identity

Visual identity stood out in a crowded CRM space.

"StreetSight shows how billboard advertising can be simplified for smaller agencies. Its clarity, bold design, and real-time insights make it a refreshing alternative to traditional workflows."
