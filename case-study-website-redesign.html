Case Study

Stallions Website Redesign

A modern marketing site focused on clearer program discovery and faster registration.

Team

Client project

Duration

6 weeks

Platform

Responsive website

Project Overview

Clear programs, faster registrations.

The Stallions Cricket Academy needed a refreshed website that made programs easier to understand and registration easier to complete. The redesign focused on clarity, strong calls to action, and trust-building content.

The challenge

Visitors struggled to quickly find the right program and complete registration. The site needed a clearer structure and more persuasive content.

  • Programs were difficult to compare
  • Registration flow had friction
  • Trust signals were weak on mobile

Research insights

Parents and players want to know class levels, schedules, and coaching credibility quickly.

  • Clear program hierarchy
  • Visible pricing and schedule cues
  • Stronger credibility and testimonials

The solution

A refreshed layout with clear program groupings, strong CTA placement, and a simplified registration path.

Key Features

Designed to convert and build trust

Program Clarity

Programs are grouped with clear levels, schedules, and benefits to reduce decision time.

Registration Flow

CTA placement and simplified form steps make it easier to complete sign-ups.

Trust & Credibility

Testimonials, coaching highlights, and event visuals build confidence on mobile.

Visual direction

A bright, sporty palette and bold typography reinforce energy and community, while the layout stays clean and easy to scan.

Results & Validation

What improved

Smoother journey

Reduced steps to find the right program.

Clearer CTAs

Higher visibility of registration actions.

Mobile ready

Responsive layout optimized for parents on the go.

My role

Web & Multimedia Designer

  • UX audit and content structure
  • UI refresh and visual assets
  • Marketing visuals and coordination

Duration: 6 weeks

Takeaways

Clear hierarchy and confident CTAs made the biggest difference in guiding visitors to act.
